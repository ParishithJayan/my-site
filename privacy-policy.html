Privacy Policy

How Coding for Civil Engineers collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: April 19, 2025

1. Who We Are

Coding for Civil Engineers (CfCE) is an online education platform operated by Parishith Jayan, offering courses on STAAD.Pro automation using the OpenSTAAD API. Our website is codingforcivilengineers.com.

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at jparishith@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact form submissions: Your name, email address, and any message or query you submit through our contact form.
  • Payment information: When you purchase a course, your payment is processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card details. Razorpay may collect and process your payment data as per their own privacy policy.
  • Course enrolment: Name and email address submitted during the enrolment process.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and questions.
  • Process your course enrolment and payment.
  • Send you course materials, session links, and updates related to your enrolment.
  • Communicate important changes to our services or policies.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Razorpay: Payment processing. Governed by Razorpay's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Font delivery. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Telegram: Internal notification system. Contact form submissions are forwarded to us via Telegram. Your data is not stored by Telegram beyond message delivery.

5. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or any advertising tracking tools.

6. Data Retention

We retain your contact form submissions and enrolment information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. If you would like your data deleted, contact us at jparishith@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at jparishith@gmail.com.

8. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of information you transmit to us.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for working professionals and are not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

  • Email: jparishith@gmail.com
  • Website: codingforcivilengineers.com

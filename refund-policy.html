Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our policy on refunds, cancellations, and course deferrals.

Last updated: April 19, 2025

We offer a 7-day refund window from the date of enrolment, provided course access has not yet commenced. Once the first live session has taken place, refunds are not available.

1. Nature of the Service

Coding for Civil Engineers (CfCE) offers live, cohort-based online courses. Our current programme, the OpenSTAAD Bootcamp for Everyone, is a fully digital service comprising live sessions, session recordings, and digital course materials.

Because our courses involve a fixed cohort schedule, instructor preparation, and limited seats, our refund policy reflects the commitments made on both sides when you enrol.

2. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 7 calendar days of your enrolment date, and
  • The first live session of your cohort has not yet taken place.

Refund requests made after the first live session has commenced will not be entertained, regardless of attendance.

3. No Refund Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • The 7-day refund window has passed.
  • The first live session has already taken place.
  • You are unable to attend sessions due to personal schedule conflicts, technical issues on your end, or work commitments.
  • You have accessed session recordings or course materials.
  • You change your mind after the refund window has closed.

4. Cohort Cancellation by Us

In the unlikely event that we cancel a cohort (due to insufficient enrolment, instructor illness, or force majeure), you will be offered:

  • A full refund to your original payment method, or
  • A guaranteed seat in the next available cohort at no additional cost.

We will notify you via email as soon as possible in such a situation.

5. Cohort Deferral

If you are unable to attend your enrolled cohort and request a deferral before the first session, we will make reasonable efforts to accommodate you in the next available cohort. Deferral is subject to seat availability and is at our discretion. Deferrals are not guaranteed.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the eligible window:

  • Email us at jparishith@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request — [Your Name]".
  • Include your name, email address used at enrolment, and payment reference number.

We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days. Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method via Razorpay.

7. Payment Gateway Charges

Razorpay may charge a transaction fee on payments. In the event of a refund, this fee may not be refundable as it is determined by the payment gateway. You will receive the full course amount minus any non-recoverable payment gateway charges.

8. Contact

For any questions about this policy, reach us at:

  • Email: jparishith@gmail.com
  • Website: codingforcivilengineers.com
